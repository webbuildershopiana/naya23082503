COVID
COVID Emergency Relief Work: -It was a matter of great honour for us to be part of meeting called by Chief Minister Office on 20th March 2020 chaired by Shri Ashok Gehlot to discuss about the Covid situation and about the steps needs to take with 40 civil societies heads. Our founder Mr Akhilesh Maheshwari represented Naya Sawera Ngo in meeting. In that meeting CM Sir has asked all the Ngo’s & Social Workers to play important role in this time of Crises. After the meeting we made the road map and served the people in pandemic.
Covid Relief Work 2021 :
1 . Oxygen Concentrator & Cylinders
2 . Hospital Beds & Blood Plasma Donations
3 . Dry Ration
4 . Sanitary Pads
5 . Food Packets
6 . Sanitizers, Soap & Mask
Keeping the crises of oxygen shortage Naya Sawera Ngo bought Oxygen Concentrator & Cylinders to provide home isolated covid patients.
Our volunteer team is regularly working on providing on call support to arrange beds in hospital by making daily basis database and also coordinating for plasma donation.
We were able to serve dry ration to Around 2000 Families who were not having any source of bread & butter including local artist, daily wage earner, auto rickshaw drivers, labourer’s. We were the Ngo partner of Reliance Foundation also in Jaipur for ration distribution Program.
Keeping the importance & necessity of Sanitary Pads for growing girls & Ladies we run the drive in which we have distributed Around 2.5 Lakh Pads in whole Rajasthan and this work was covered by many News Channels & Newspapers.
We have also distributed Around 5,000 Cook Food packets by the help of various Restaurants & individuals.
We have distributed around 2000 Hygiene Kit of Small bottle of Sanitizer, Small Soap & Mask.
