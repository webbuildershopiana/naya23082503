About
Us
We Provide Nonprofit
Service Since 2009
‘Naya Sawera’ is one of the leading Youth Base, registered Non-Government & Not-For-Profit Charitable Organization registered under Societies Registration Act working for the welfare of the people of different underprivileged sections of society by various means. The aim is to see that no human being is deprived of his basic needs because of poverty. The organization also seeks to support and nurture the talents who cannot avail proper facilities due to their harsh Conditions...
Our Mission
Imagine living in a loving, joyful society where all members of the community accept people with disabilities as members of their family and where everyone has the freedom to live their lives to the fullest. This is the kind of world we want to build by establishing one big, happy family that values each girl and protects the environment.
Our Strength
Being a youth based NGO, Naya Sawera believes that its power lies with the young volunteers that work with the organization. The young volunteers and core team members have the freedom of making their own decision regarding the NGO. This lends fresher perspective and better execution to the projects and campaigns run by the NGO. This not only smooth-ens the function of our organization, but gives the volunteer a head start for a more independent and critically equipped future.
Join Us
Imagine a world full of love and happiness where whole society accepts especially challenged kids and people as part of their family, where each individual gets equal right to live and enjoy each moment of the life that our mission to create one family surrounded by green world respecting each girl and nurturing the environment.
Projects &
Campaign
“Snehanchal” – Love, Care, Celebration
Snehanchal, an open shelter home for boys, was an initiative taken by the Naya Sawera Society 4 years ago. The heart of the organization, this project is based on providing shelter to young boys who are displaced (go missing), rescued from child labor, and/or orphaned. Till now, the organization has helped around 520 children
“Sarthak”
In order to help the lonely, helpless and destitute elders the Naya Sawera Society opened “Sarthak” old age home in 2022. At present, it accommodates 12 needy elders.
“Atmanirbhar”
In January 2023, three enthusiastic young volunteers initiated Project Atmanirbhar, with the primary objective of providing employment opportunities to financially needy women and equipping them with tailoring skills. What started as a humble training venture has now blossomed into a small enterprise
“Kokh“ – Pehla Kadam
Under this campaign, we are taking care of the pregnant ladies so as to decrease the Maternal Mortality Rate in India. We are giving them proper vitamin and nutrition for the first 9 months. We have also extended this facility to one month after the delivery.
